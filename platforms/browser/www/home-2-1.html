Cerner's mission is to contribute to the systemic improvement of health care delivery and the health of communities.

Cerner Middle East has been operating in the Middle East for 25 years, and has a proven track record in the region, working with more than 200 client facilities that range from large government hospital networks to health clinics.

Cerner Millennium solutions can be found in United Arab Emirates, Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, Egypt, Qatar and Kuwait, putting Cerner Middle East on the front lines of health care transformation. Building on our industry-leading clinical technology expertise and vast global experience, we continue to develop new and innovative ways to deliver value to our clients, while addressing the unique challenges of each country we work in.

Cerner Millennium enables:

  • Evidence-based decision making for clinicians
  • Reduced medication variances through streamlined administration and dispensing
  • Improving care for common high-risk conditions
  • Increased staff efficiency and patient care with real-time bed management
  • Decreased turnaround time for results management and notes retrieval
  • Greater collaboration and handover of information between clinicians and staff
  • Enhanced care management through clinical real time clinical dashboards

With offices in UAE, Qatar, Saudi Arabia and Egypt; Cerner is able to provide the direct support and partnership with our client base. Within the Dubai based office is Application Management Services™ (AMS) team who offers 24x7x365 remote application management that features proactive monitoring, system management, and world- class level 2 production support of Cerner Millennium solutions and experienced resources. The AMS offerings paired with superior customer service allows:

  • Clients’ Millennium solutions to run more efficiently,
  • A more comprehensive use of applications for greater solution adoption and deeper system usage,
  • Clients to be more strategic to focus on their roadmap, projects and initiatives.
