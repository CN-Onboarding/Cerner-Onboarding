Week 13 - Dubai, UAE
  • Brief Summary
    Time What to Do
    1.5 hours Weekly Associate 1:1 with Manager
    • Associate welfare/happiness/issues
    • Role Progress/Questions (ensure you have spoken to people working with associate to get feedback)
    • Training Progress (If training ask associate how affective they are finding the training, is there anything that needs changing)
    • Accommodation Search (discuss they are 2 weeks into their 4 week hotel allocation in Dubai)
    • Corporate training completion within 45 days of start date (ensure the associate is on track to completed the training within the 45 days)
    • Role based uLearn Training progress (review their progress against the expected progress)
    Remainder of week Role Based Training/Work Assignment as per Managers 100 day plan
    30 minutes Meet with HR to ensure the associate is happy, has questions or concerns

    Manager to Liaise with Consulting Operations to identify a suitable client site visit to take place in Week 6, including organized transport and escort from member of the EM team.
    1 hour (Thursday) Thursday weekly touch point with buddy to run through full plan for following week to check if any questions/concerns
